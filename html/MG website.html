The Kakapo Chalet is the largest chalet of the three and it has a large kitchen, dining and living area. The Kakapo chalet has great amenities. The Kakapo Chalet is able to accommodate a maximum of 10 adults and 30 children.

Pūkeko.

The Pukeko Chalet is great for large groups or families that are wanting chalet to themselves but to ensure a comfortable, a limit to the number of children and adults must be set. Become a Club Member

In order to hire the chalets available, sign up to become a club member. All you need to do is to provide your full name, address, and proof of both your physical address (eg. photo of power bill) and your id (eg. driver's license or passport).
